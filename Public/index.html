Take the Pledge

In order to create a welcoming and secure culture for my customers/employees I pledge to do and instruct my empolyees to do the following:

  • Use either multicultural or secular decorations.
  • Use the phrase "Happy Holidays" unless addressing someone known to celebrate a specific holiday.
  • Take the concerns of customers and employees seriously and attempt to address them in a way that is equitable for everyone in the community.
